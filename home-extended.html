Founder / CEO

As the founder of multiple startups, including an autonomous delivery robot company acquired by Caterpillar in 2020, he has raised significant funding and led world-class teams. With a background in computer science from Carnegie Mellon, his career spans roles at Apple and Google, emphasizing his deep technical expertise and passion for transformative innovation.
